Frontend Engineer

As a part of growing front-end team, you will work with designers to constantly improving user experience. You will also collaborate with others throughout our organization and technology stack to build products that are revolutionizing the way our clients achieve their business goals.

Responsibilities

  • Build out new projects from scratch and maintain/improve existing ones
  • Evolve our UI component library, standards, and best practices to streamline development and ensure consistency across existing products
  • Collaborate with product, engineering, and design to iterate on experiments and product features that immediately impact our clients' customers
  • Participate in design and code reviews
  • Lead cross-functional initiatives and mentor and guide our engineering staff in the development of new and existing frontend technology

Requirements

  • You’ve got 5 years of experience with Browser Extensions, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and React
  • Outstanding attention to the details and pixel-perfect approach
  • You’re a product thinker who cares about the customer
  • You have high accountability and ownership of your work
  • You’re comfortable to do whatever it takes, even if this means working outside of your role

Lead UX/UI designer

We're looking for a Lead UX/UI designer to join our team. You will be responsible for creating intuitive, visually appealing and effective designs for our clients.

Responsibilities

  • Lead product design through active design sessions, work review, and feedback
  • Work with the product and engineering team to develop, implement and manage a transparent design process
  • Work closely with UX to develop elegant, responsive, and thoughtful designs based on user research that considers user needs and technical constraints
  • Manage the work output of a single designer
  • Use an iterative approach where you take the design from wireframe to sketches and then through to high fidelity interactive prototypes for validation
  • Work across all stages of the design process, from gathering customer feedback to producing exceptional designs to implementation with our engineering team

Requirements

  • Exceptional skills in visual design with a keen focus on user experience. The ability to architect information and craft designs in a way that is not only beautiful but contributes to elevated user experience. This includes exceptional attention to detail and pixel perfect designs
  • 5+ years experience designing digital products, preferably SaaS, web applications and eCommerce
  • 2+ years experience in a design-centric leadership role, with at least 1-2 direct reports
  • Demonstrable experience and capabilities in design leadership, including advanced understanding of UX/UI and establishing a product design practice
  • Goes beyond merely presenting designs, explains the story of the rationale (preferably evidence-based) and benefits of design ideas
  • Understanding of technical and business constraints with the ability to adapt the design process accordingly

Junior UX/UI Designer

In this position, you will take a hands-on role as a UI Engineer Intern on one of our product teams. For the most part, you will learn by doing: daily collaborating with our art-director, lead designer, frontend and backend engineers on your product team.

Responsibilities

  • Designing interfaces that make the difference in quality, usability, and visual impact
  • Support other designers in executing projects already in production
  • Collaborating with the product team on all design stages - starting from the research and finishing with the developers' support

Requirements

  • Strong knowledge of Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD
  • Basic typography, graphics, and interaction design skills
  • Basic knowledge of composition, color, imagery, typography
  • Understanding of UI/UX thinking and overall CX
  • Detail oriented with the ability to organize, manage time and expectations
  • Team player that is not afraid to speak their mind
  • Basic knowledge and understanding of CSS and HTML
